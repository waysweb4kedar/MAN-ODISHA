Workshops/Seminars

17th May 2010: A SEMINAR

A  seminar-cum-interactive session with parents was conducted on 17th May headed by Mrs. Nalini Menon ( Head , Rainbow Centre for Autism, Bangalore) Mrs. Inderjeet Khurana (Chairperson, Ruchika High School), Mrs. Bimaljeet Dua , Principal  of this school), teachers & Special Educators of different schools, parents & staff of Autism Therapy Centre attended and discussed on various behavioral problems and issues of special kids.
It was a fruitful session.

2nd -5th September 2010:
PARENT’S TRAINING PROGRAMME.

This programme was held from 2nd September to 5th September at Future Bhubaneswar School (FBS)
It was headed by Mrs. Nalini Menon ( Head , Rainbow Centre for Autism , Bangalore), and attended by  teachers,  Special Educators, parents & staff (both teaching and non-teaching)  of FBS ,other schools and Autism Therapy Centre to orient themselves for the Inclusive Education  programme.
Quite a good number of parents with special kids made an assessment and management strategy of their special kids.

 26.02.2011 : SEMINAR ON  S.I.

A seminar-cum-interactive session with parents was conducted on 26th Feb’11. It was  headed by Mr. Anurupa Senapati, H.O.D. department of occupational therapy, SVNIRTAR, Olatpur.  and attended by  teachers of FBS,  Special Educators, parents  of Autism Therapy Centre,  teachers of Cambridge School,Cuttack, Special educaters of  Sahaya Centre and Ashraya Centre of Cuttack. The discussion was mainly on various Sensory issues of not only special kids but kids enrolled in normal schools having difficulties in writing, memorizing, socializing with peer groups etc. The meeting was presided by Mr.Thomas , Principal of FBS. Among other guests were Mr.P.R.Dash, Mr. Madan Mohan Panda, Trustee, Mrs. Pravati Mishra, Director, TCTD, Mr. Girish Das, Trustee  and Mrs. Swarna lata Mishra, Chairperson of MAN Trust.

It was indeed a fruitful session.

10.12.2011: ANNUAL FUNCTION of Autism Therapy Centre

Autism Therapy Centre (ATC) , Bhubaneswar which is run by the Manage Autism Now (MAN) Trust observed its Autism Awareness Day cum Annual Day on 10.12.2011(Saturday) in Future Bhubaneswar School (FBS) near Sikharchandi. The function was graced by Mr. Devendra Prasad, G.M SBI  as the Chief Guest and  Mr. Satakadi Hota, well known  writer and Editor of Samay as the President. In his address Mr. Devendra Prasad, Chief Guest  mentioned that the centre was doing a laudable job and deserves support and encouragement from all concerned. These special kids need to be treated with love, compassion and care so that their special talents are provided a conducive atmosphere to flourish, enabling them to integrate with the society. Mr. Madan Mohan Panda, Trustee of MAN Trust and Mrs. Swarna lata Mishra, Chairperson of the MAN Trust also addressed the gathering .Mr. Sanjiv Nayak, Director Silicon Institute also Trustee  of MAN Trust attended the function. On this occasion a cultural programme was also organized. The special kids of ATC and staffs of the centre presented physical activities, song, yoga and dance.
It was indeed a fruitful function.

10.03.2012: SEMINAR ON PARENT COUNSELING

A  seminar-cum-interactive session with parents was conducted on 10th March’12  headed by  Mr.Suvendu Das, General Manager (HR), Paradeep Phosphet Limited (PPL)  as  Resource person and attended by  parents  ,  Special Educators  of   Autism Therapy Centre,  parents  of  different  Autism centers of Bhubaneswar and Cuttack. The meeting was presided by Mr. Madan Mohan Panda , Trustee  of  MAN Trust . Among others   were Mr.P.R.Dash, , Trustee, Mr  Sanjeev Nayak , Trustee  and Mrs.  Swarna lata Mishra,   Chairperson   of   MAN Trust, Mr. Sharma, Director,VRC, Ms. C. Vijaya, Ms. S.Satpathy , educationists    and  a   good number  of   persons   related  to the special field. The discussion was mainly on various Social   issues of  special  kids  and   adults   and   how  to  handle   these   with  developing   proper  awareness , patience , attitude.

It was indeed a fruitful session.

To accelerate the process of socialization and bring the special kids into the mainstream, we not only celebrate various occasions like Independence Day, Republic Day, Teacher’s Day, Children’s Day and Autism Awareness Day ,Saraswati Puja , Ganesh Puja but also  take the special kids of the therapy centre on regular outings (at least once in a month )to various places of interest like Dhauli , Nandankanan, Important temples, Parks, Malls etc.
It may be pertinent to mention that one of the kids of A.T.C. namely CHINU (Gautam Panda) had the proud privilege of his photograph figuring in the calendar of 2009 published by NATIONAL TRUST.
